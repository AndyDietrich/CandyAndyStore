1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Andix Games ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares data when you play Candy Andy ("the Game"). We respect your privacy and comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

By using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly from players. However, when you use the Game, certain data may be collected automatically by third-party service providers integrated into the Game.

These include:

  • Google AdMob — to deliver advertisements, including personalized and rewarded ads.
  • Facebook SDK — for advertising performance and campaign tracking.
  • Unity Analytics — to analyze gameplay behavior and performance metrics.
  • Google Play Services — to support achievements, leaderboards, and general app functionality.

These services may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • IP address and device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Usage data (game events, session length, in-app interactions)
  • General location (country or region)

We do not process names, email addresses, or contact details of users.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, processing by our third-party partners is based on:

  • Legitimate interests (for analytics and service improvement)
  • User consent (for personalized advertising, where applicable)

You can withdraw consent for personalized ads via your device settings.

4. How Data Is Used

Data collected via third-party SDKs may be used to:

  • Deliver ads and manage ad frequency
  • Offer rewarded video features
  • Measure campaign effectiveness
  • Analyze usage trends and improve gameplay experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above for the purposes described.

For more details, please review their policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Unity Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services Privacy

6. Data Retention

We do not store or manage personal data. Retention is determined by our third-party service providers according to their policies.

7. Data Transfers

Third-party partners may transfer and process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Such transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards under GDPR.

8. Children's Privacy

Candy Andy is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or applicable local minimum age). AdMob's child-directed settings are enabled to ensure age-appropriate ads.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

You may exercise the following rights regarding your personal data processed by our third-party partners:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to certain uses
  • Data portability (where applicable)

To make such a request, contact us at support@candyandy.store. We will forward your inquiry to the appropriate service provider(s).

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the Game and its data integrations from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will take effect upon publication in the Game or on its official app store listing.
